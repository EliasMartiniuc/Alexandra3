About Forever Living

The Aloe Vera Company. For over 40 years, we have dedicated ourselves to seeking out nature's best sources for health and beauty and sharing them with the world.

Our Leadership

Rex Maughan founded Forever Living Products in 1978. He had a vision of bringing the power of Aloe Vera to people all over the world.

Today, Forever Living is a multi-billion dollar company operating in over 160 countries.

Plant to Product

We own our own aloe fields, manufacturing facilities, research and development, quality control laboratories and even our own distribution channels.

This allows us to control the cost and quality of every step of the process.
